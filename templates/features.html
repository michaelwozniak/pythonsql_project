WHAT

CV_Cleaner removes unneeded images from your dataset, which can later be used to train machine learning algorithms. If your dataset consists of 2 or more separable classes of images, you can cluster them and then download into separable folders using our tool (e.g. by receiving a dataset from the Vienna Zoo we want to perform segmentation that will divide the set into animals and their food, and runs). Our solution is fast, precise and robust in image clustering.



FOR WHOM

This tool is designed for anyone who works in the field of Computer Vision. If you professionally deal with Data Science, ML Engineering or are a student - you will definitely need this tool and you will love it.
